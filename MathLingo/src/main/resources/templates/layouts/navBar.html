How to Play

Welcome to Integrordle, a Worlde-inspired game where your only goal is to correctly type the inner function of the integral within the alloted number of guesses.


While this game is similar to Wordle, there are a couple key differences to note.


In the game, any character is its own separate box meaning each box will contain 1 letter OR 1 mathematical symbol (+, -, /, (, )) OR 1 number.


Boxes smaller than the others and raised up are exponents.


Good luck and Happy Integrordling!
